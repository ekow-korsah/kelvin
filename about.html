NICE TO MEET YOU.

NICE TO MEET YOU.

Hey there, I'm Kelvin! I am an entry level user experience designer from Ghana, West Africa. I gravitate toward projects that require a high level of accessibility, usability, and visual polish. In my view, the best product is created by closely interacting with users and stakeholders throughout the design process. I enjoy taking complex problems and transforming them into simple beautiful designs. Because their pleasure is my delight, I truly love involving my users at every stage of the process. I've always liked how thoughtful design can make people's lives easier all throughout the world. I enjoy making things easier for people, whether it's by simplifying one interaction or allowing them to see things from a different perspective. I enjoy working on projects that focus on delivering a usable, equitable, enjoyable, and useful user experience. I've previously worked as a UX Design job shadow and completed five of the seven courses in the Google UX Design certificate course on Coursera. When I'm not designing, you'll find me practicing photography, watching NBA games, playing basketball, playing the piano, or working on the next Marvel movie.
